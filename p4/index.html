How to Play

Begin by choosing who will who will be Player X and who will be Player O. Player X will start first. Next, take turns clicking on a 3x3 grid until someone gets three X's or three O's in a row, or ultimately filling the whole board.

Three possible outcomes each game:

  • Three X's in a row (diagonally, horizontally, or vertically) - Plaer X wins!
  • Three O's in a row (diagonally, horizontally, or vertically) - Player O wins!
  • If neither can get three of a kind in a row and the nine boxes are filled - The game results in a Tie!

What you'll need:

  • An opponent
  • Desire to win
  • You!
